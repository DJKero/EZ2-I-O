Port 0x103: P1 Turntable
	1 Byte:
		0-255 range

Port 0x104: P2 Turntable
	1 Byte:
		0-255 range

Port 0x101: Starts + Effectors
	1 Byte:
		TEST SERVICE E4 E3 E2 E1 P2 P1

Port 0x102: B1~B5 + Pdl 1
	1 Byte:
		PEDAL QE QE B5 B4 B3 B2 B1

Port 0x106: B6~B10 + Pdl 2
	1 Byte:
		PEDAL QE QE B10 B9 B8 B7 B6

Ports 0x102 and 0x106:
	QE bits act as a quadrature encoder:
		TT byte value - QE bits value
		           0x8F-00
		           0x8F-01
		           0x8F-11
		           0x8F-10
		           0x90-00
	    (thanks Ziemas for the input!)
